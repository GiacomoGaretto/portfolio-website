Salotto di Milano

The Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II in the metaverse

This project was developed for the Anthropology of Communication course in the Master's program in Communication Design at Politecnico di Milano. It is part of the "CityVerse" concept, which involves bringing elements of the urban fabric into the metaverse to enable digital interaction and expand its potential applications.

The Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II in Milan was chosen as a symbolic element of the city and for its floor plan, which is divided into four sections. The 3D model was personally created in Blender, aiming to simplify its forms while preserving the architectural charm of the gallery.

Using the online platform "Spatial.io" and its custom editor on Unity, the gallery was integrated into a digital world. Within this space, an interactive experience was designed for the player, whose goal is to collect elements that provide information about the gallery.

The four sections of the gallery each represent a different historical period.
A custom texture was created for each section so that, once the exploration is completed, its appearance changes from its present-day look to a historically projected version.

The brand identity of Salotto di Milano is designed around the shapes of the Galleria, viewed from above in an orthogonal projection.
