Chronicles of Ink

Tattoo's double standards trough a Digital Narrative experience.

The Chronicles of Ink is an Interactive Digital Narrative experience that explores social judgement and self-exploration through the metaphorical fantasy world of Talea. The project aims to raise awareness of the social double standard towards tattoos by examining how the perception of these art forms varies culturally and socially.

Talea is an environment structured around four factions, where each one represents a unique approach to tattoos, from celebrating traditions to encouraging individualism and inclusivity, to rigid judgement and social conformity. The Chronicles of Ink promotes reflection and self-exploration through interactions with characters who embody contrasting values, providing a safe environment to explore one’s own prejudices and values.

The central goal within the Talea is the journey towards self-discovery. The user’s choices will have a direct effect on their avatar as a testimony of the choices made.

Within this realm, four major factions emerge, each embodying distinct ideologies and principles: The Order of Recognition seeks acknowledgment and clarity, while The Coalition of Acceptance advocates for inclusivity and harmony.
The Vanguard of Rebellion embodies defiance and change, whereas The Circle of Judgment represents evaluation and categorization.
Each faction is represented by its own Guardian.

A construction grid system was developed to create the symbols and shapes for each faction. The combination of values from each faction generates a unique symbol based on the player's choices.

The six characters that the player will encounter during their experience are placed on the map based on their belonging to each faction.

Each character has a unique set of items that the player must collect to access specific dialogue sections with their owners, influencing the outcome of the experience.

During the dialogue, the player can observe how their choices influence their belonging to each faction's values and how this also affects their appearance.

After interacting with one character in each section of the map, the player will complete the experience. A page displaying the final shape and the experience stats will appear.
