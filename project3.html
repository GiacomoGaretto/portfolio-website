VOTE

Today for tomorrow

The project was born from the need to engage young people in the value of democratic participation. Research revealed a growing disengagement from voting and representation, with an increasing number of young people choosing not to participate. Key factors behind this trend include a lack of accessible information, political disinterest, and the perception that voting is ineffective.

To address these challenges, VOTE was developed, a system that transforms democratic participation into an active and engaging experience. Through a web app and interactive monitors installed in schools, students can discuss current issues and experience the voting process firsthand. The system collects and visualizes personalized data using an original design system, offering insights into individual and collective perspectives. At the end of each session, a summary presents the week's results, encouraging deeper reflection on the meaning of voting.

Empowering the voters of tomorrow through an engaging and interactive experience, where learning and voting thrive together.

The project aims to make voting an engaging and educational experience for high school students, turning democracy from an abstract idea into concrete action.
It highlights the importance of discussion and representation through an interactive system that guides students in exploration, debate, and informed decision-making.

To make the learning process natural and engaging, this section is designed based on the dynamics of popular social platforms among young users. Each day, relevant topics are presented in a Reel-style format, alternating between audio sources, featuring expert insights and text-based content, such as selected news articles and blogs. Students can intuitively browse through content, explore different perspectives, and dive deeper into key topics. Every source can be saved for later reference or shared with other users, encouraging discussion and information exchange. This approach seamlessly integrates learning with daily digital habits, transforming information into an interactive and participatory experience.

This vision shapes the design choices: a clear and intuitive interface balances authority with engagement, using dynamic visuals to encourage interaction.
A modular structure ensures seamless accessibility across devices, from the web app to school monitors. Animations and transitions enhance the sense of progression and active participation, reinforcing a cohesive visual identity aligned with the project's mission.

The avatar interface represents the user's personal development within the platform, tracking their participation and growth. Every choice carries weight and contributes to the evolution of the avatar, making the impact of decisions visible.

This section clearly displays user activity data, including:
Voting history with daily selected options
Total number of collected sources, including text articles and audio content
Total number of interactions, such as comments, likes, and initiated threads

At the end of the experience, students receive a comprehensive summary of the data collected during the activity. The results are presented in a format inspired by Spotify Wrapped, a familiar reference for young users, featuring engaging visuals and easy-to-read insights. Key highlights include the most active class, the most debated topic, and voting trends throughout the week, providing a clear overview of the experience and its impact. This final moment concludes the journey, encouraging reflection on emerging trends and reinforcing a sense of community among students.
