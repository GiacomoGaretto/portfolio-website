Falken's Room

This thesis was carried out with the aim of analyzing, through the creation of a practical case study, the characteristics of interactive installations, highlighting their development path, communicative potential and critical issues. The case study was the design of a three-dimensional interactive installation centered on the world of arcade video games from the 80s, set up and used by the public on the occasion of the Graphic Days 2022 festival.
